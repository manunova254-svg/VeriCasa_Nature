What are farming methods?
These are techniques adopted in either field cultivation or livestock rearing.

Which ones do you already know?

  1. Shift-cultivation.
    2. The practice of farming on a piece of land till its nutrient supply is depleted then relocating to a more fertile land.
  2. Mixed farming
    3. the practice of growing crops and rearing of livestocks on the same piece of land.
  3. Agro-forestry
    4. The practice of growing crops and trees on same piece of land
  4. Organic farming
    5. The practice of doing farming without the use of agro-chemicals.
