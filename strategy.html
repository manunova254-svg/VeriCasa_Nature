1. Industry Context & Strategic Opportunity

Macroeconomic Importance
  • ~12% of Kenya’s GDP
  • 47% of agricultural GDP
  • ~50% workforce employment
  • 47M sheep & goats, 15–18M cattle, 38–57M poultry
Demand-Supply Gap
  • Meat deficit: ~300,000 MT
  • Feed deficit: ~60%
  • Post-harvest loss: ~46%
  • Feed prices ↑ ~37%

2. Agro-Business Development Model

Integrated Value Chain: Feed Production → Livestock → Infrastructure → Processing → Market Linkage

3. Disease & Parasite Mitigation

  • Biosecurity: restricted access, footbaths, quarantine
  • Vaccination: FMD, LSD, Newcastle, PPR
  • Deworming every 3 months
  • Tick control via spraying/dipping
  • AI monitoring for early detection
